Membership



Memebership in April Sound Homeowners Civic Assocation (ASCA) is limited to April Sound property owners. ASCA membership is strictly voluntary and its membership enrollment process is seperate from that of April Sound Property owners Association and April Sound Country Club. Annual membership dues are:


  • Individual Membership $15
  • Family Membership $25
