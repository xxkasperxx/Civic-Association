Leadership




The Affairs of ASCA are managed by a Board of Directors. Directors are elected each year at the annual membership meeting helf on the first Friday of March at 7:00 pm at the April Sound Country Club. At each year's annual meeting, the current board presents a slate of candidates to replace dicretors with expiring terms of office. The slate is approved by the ASCA members in attendance at the annual meeting. The 13 board members each serve a two year term of office, which expires on a staggered basis.


After the annual meeting the board of dicretors meet to select officers to lead ASCA.
