Directory



ASCA provides a free April Sound Directory to each household in April Sound every two years. The directory is funded through sale of ads, which appear in the dicretory.

The 2010-2013 directory was distributed to each April Sound home in Febuary 2012. The next directory will be published and distibuted in Febuary 2012.

ASCA must have your premission to list your name and current information as you want it to be listed in the directory. Although you man have been listed in the previous directory, ASCA must have your premission to list your name and information in the new directory.
