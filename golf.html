Golf Tournament



The April Sound Civic Association (ASCA) is preparing for our 11th annual Golf Tournament scheduled for Sunday, April 22, 2012, at the April Sound Country Club, 1000 April Sound Blvd, Montgomery, Texas. A putting contest will be held during registration from 11:30 am - 12:45 pm. Shot gun start begins at 1:00 pm. Cash prizes will be presented at the awards dinner and silent auction following the tournament. Team format is 4 person scramble. Golfers may choose their team of 4 or may request to be placed on a team. Tournament fees, which include greens fees and carts are:

  • Individual player: ASCA Member            $60
  • Individual player: non-ASCA Member     $75
  • Awards dinner only                                 $25

    • Proceeds from the tournament will be used for ASCA projects in the April Sound Community. To ensure the tournament's success, ASCA seeks both sponsor contributions and silent auction donors. Sponsors recieve recognition with invdividual signage at specifc holes. During the awards presentation following the tournament, both sponsors and silent auction donors recieve special regonition.
