Incorporated i 1977 as a 501(c) 3 non-profit organization, April Sound Homeownsers Civic Assocation's (ASCA) mission is to engage in beautification and recreational projects in April Sound that will encahnce the quality of life and improve property values.

ASCA works closely with both the April Sound Property owners Association (POA) and April Sound Country Club. With the common goal of serving the April Sound residents, all thee organizations collaborate on beautification projects,recreational projects and other initiatives.

However, ASCA, the POA and the Club are three separate, distinct legal organizations. ASCA has its own bylaws, board of directors, federal tax identifcation number, bank account, and membership enrollment.
